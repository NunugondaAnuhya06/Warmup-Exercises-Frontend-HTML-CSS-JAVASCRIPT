Introduction to Attributes of Font Tags in Formatting Tags

"hr Tags consists of 4 types of Atrributes :"

1.color=".." [used to specify the horizantal rule color]

2.width=".." [used to specify the width of horizantal rule]

3.align=".." [used to specify the alignment LEFT/RIGHT/CENTER] Default is center

4.size=".." [used to specify the thickness of horizantal rule]
